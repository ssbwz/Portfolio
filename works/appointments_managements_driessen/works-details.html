Driessen Appointment Management Application

Software Engineering - 2022

I collaborated with an agile team to develop an appointment management application for Driessen, a recruitment company. My role involved integrating the application with Azure using Microsoft Graph API.

Our aim was to provide a user-friendly solution that simplifies the appointment scheduling process for recruiters and candidates. By integrating with Microsoft Graph API, we could access Azure resources such as calendars and email, resulting in a highly functional and secure solution.

Through this project, I gained valuable experience in software development, agile methodologies, and Microsoft Graph API integration. Our appointment management application for Driessen is an innovative solution that enhances productivity and efficiency in the recruitment industry.

Features

  • User authentication: The application allows recruiters/admins to log in and out of their accounts securely.
  • Candidate management: Recruiters/admins can view all assigned candidates per vacancy and choose which ones to accept or reject.
  • Appointment management: Recruiters/admins can view the details of appointments and switch the designated recruiter for an appointment. They can also create appointments for candidates and candidates can schedule appointments with recruiters.
  • Overview pages: The application provides an overview of all appointments and vacancies to recruiters/admins.

Demonstration

Scheduling a meeting

When a candidate schedules an appointment through the Driessen appointment management application, the scheduling page displays available appointment times based on the recruiter's availability, which is synced with their Microsoft calendar. The candidate selects a preferred appointment time. Once the appointment is scheduled, the recruiter receives an email notification and the appointment is automatically added to their Microsoft calendar. The candidate receives a confirmation email containing the details of the appointment.

Vacancies

On the vacancies page, recruiters/admins can view a list of available vacancies along with important information such as the vacancy title and address. By clicking on the "More information" button for a specific vacancy, they can view a list of candidates who have been invited for the initial meeting related to that vacancy.

Appointments

On the Appointments page, recruiters/admins can view all upcoming appointments and their corresponding details, such as the recruiter's name and meeting location. By using the "Change Recruiter" button, recruiters/admins can switch the designated recruiter for an appointment if the original recruiter became unavailable at that time.
